About Me

Welcome to my website!

Let me introduce myself. My name is Carl Chilton. I'm currently studying to become a full stack web developer. This website is the first of many websites, following the course content covering front end web development.

A little bit about my career history, I worked as a desktop and network engineer for 10 years, where I first properly encountered coding. I have always enjoyed it. Following a brief tour of south east asia, I came back to the UK and started working in Service Delivery, providing helpdesk services for government contracts. Throughout this period, I heavily utilised code, such as VBA, HTML and CSS with microsoft office and SharePoint. It was at this point I made a decision to focus on coding fully and study in my spare time.

As is always the case in life when starting a new venture, I am keen to get more experience and have the proof to back it up. As such, I am more than willing to provide front end web development services for free, to be able to build up my portfolio as soon as possible. I have always felt I have a personal creative flair, which I also apply to the various musical instruments I have picked up over the years. If you, or someone you know, needs the help of these services, then please feel free to pop over to the contact section of the site and get in touch. :)
